One Punch Man

One Punch Man: The story of an ordinary man who wanted to become a superhero and decided to train everyday. His name is Saitama, and everyday he jogs 100 miles, does 100 push-ups and sit-ups. During this journey of his to become stronger, he meets all sorts of people. Even takes in a disciple. Despite his accomplishments, there's one thing he cannot change no matter what--his bald head. The price he had to pay to become extremely powerful was losing his hair and becoming bald.
